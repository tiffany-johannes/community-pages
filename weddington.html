The Town

Where Estates Meet Excellence

Weddington is a semi-rural luxury enclave in Union County, where one-acre minimums are the norm and rolling pastures share the landscape with stately custom homes. It's a place where residents put down deep roots, horses graze in the front yard, and some of North Carolina's highest-rated schools are right down the road.

Large-Lot Luxury Living

Weddington's strict zoning preserves its rural character — most lots are one acre or more. You'll find custom-built estates on sprawling acreage, mature tree canopies, and the kind of privacy and space that simply doesn't exist closer to the city.

Top-Rated Schools

Weddington Elementary, Weddington Middle, and Weddington High School are among the highest-ranked public schools in North Carolina. People relocate here specifically for the school district, and it shows in every measure of academic excellence.

Equestrian Community

Horse farms and equestrian estates are woven into Weddington's identity. The large-lot zoning supports boarding facilities, riding rings, and pastureland. If you've dreamed of a horse property within commuting distance of Charlotte, this is where you look.

Providence Country Club

Located nearby, Providence Country Club offers a premier golf, tennis, swimming, and social experience. It's a hub for the Weddington and Marvin communities, providing resort-style amenities and a vibrant neighborhood network.

Welcoming & Safe

With virtually no commercial zoning in much of the town, Weddington is defined by its residential tranquility. Crime rates are extremely low, neighbors know each other, and the community is deeply invested in keeping things exactly the way they are.

25 Minutes to Charlotte

Providence Road South (NC-16) connects Weddington to Uptown Charlotte in about 25–30 minutes. Adjacent to Waxhaw and Marvin, residents enjoy easy access to shopping and dining along the corridor while returning home to quiet, country-like surroundings.

The Case for Weddington

Why People Move Here

  • 1

    The Schools

    Weddington's public schools are consistently ranked among the best in North Carolina. People move here for Weddington High alone, and the feeder pattern from elementary through high school is one of the strongest in Union County.

  • 2

    Space and Privacy

    With one-acre minimum lot sizes across much of the town, Weddington offers the kind of breathing room that's increasingly rare near a major city. Established estates, newer luxury construction, and horse properties provide options at every scale.

  • 3

    Union County Wealth

    Union County is one of the wealthiest counties in North Carolina, and Weddington sits at its heart. The community's high household incomes support excellent public services, well-maintained infrastructure, and a strong tax base.

  • 4

    Strong Market Fundamentals

    At a median sold price around $750,000, Weddington remains a seller's market with approximately 2.5 months of inventory. Homes sell at 98.5% of list price and move in about 35 days — reflecting sustained demand for this premier address.
