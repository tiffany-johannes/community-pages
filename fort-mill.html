The Town

Where Charlotte Meets South Carolina’s Best

Fort Mill is a rapidly growing town in York County, just across the South Carolina border from Charlotte. With top-rated schools, SC tax advantages, and a mix of master-planned communities and charming established neighborhoods, Fort Mill has become one of the most sought-after addresses in the Charlotte metro.

Fort Mill School District

One of the highest-rated school districts in South Carolina. Fort Mill schools consistently earn top marks in state rankings, and the district is a primary driver for people relocating from Charlotte and across the region. It's the kind of district people move specifically to be in.

Anne Springs Close Greenway

Over 2,100 acres of protected nature preserve with more than 30 miles of trails for hiking, biking, and horseback riding. Kayaking on Lake Haigler, seasonal events, and a working farm — it's an extraordinary amenity right in the heart of Fort Mill.

Baxter Village & Kingsley

Baxter Village is a beloved mixed-use community with walkable shops, restaurants, and tree-lined streets. Kingsley Town Center brings a modern retail and dining hub to the area. These aren't just neighborhoods — they're destinations.

Tega Cay & Lake Wylie

The Tega Cay peninsula offers waterfront living on Lake Wylie with golf, parks, and a tight-knit community feel. The Gold Hill area and surrounding lakefront properties provide some of the most scenic addresses in the Charlotte metro.

Carowinds & Entertainment

Carowinds theme and water park is located right in Fort Mill, straddling the NC/SC state line. Add in a growing restaurant scene, local breweries, and community events, and there's no shortage of entertainment options close to home.

20 Minutes to Uptown Charlotte

I-77 connects Fort Mill to Uptown Charlotte in 20–25 minutes. Charlotte Douglas International Airport is about 30 minutes away. You get South Carolina living with full access to everything Charlotte has to offer.
